At APU FC, we are more than just a student organization – we are a thriving community of athletes, fans, and football enthusiasts united by our love for the beautiful game. Whether you're a competitive player, a strategic mind, or simply a passionate supporter, our club is the perfect place to channel your energy and commitment to football.

Founded with the vision of nurturing talent and promoting sportsmanship, APU FC offers a platform for personal growth, competitive spirit, and lifelong friendships. Our members come from all walks of life and nationalities, reflecting the vibrant and diverse student body of Asia Pacific University.

From intensive training sessions to thrilling tournaments, from team-building events to community outreach, every moment spent with APU FC is a step toward excellence – both on and off the field.

What You’ll Find on Our Website:

  • Join the Team – Ready to take the field? Sign up and become part of our active squad or support staff.

  • Meet the Players – Get to know the faces behind our success through detailed player profiles.

  • Stay Updated – Don’t miss out on training schedules, upcoming matches, or the latest club announcements.

  • Events & Tournaments – Discover exciting competitions and club-hosted events happening throughout the year.

  • Reach Out – Have questions or suggestions? Contact us directly through our site.
