About APU Football Club

The Heartbeat of Football at Asia Pacific University

More than just a team, the APU Football Club is a thriving, varied community bound together by a love of the great game. Since our inception, we have established a reputation for the relationships we create off the field as well as for our on-field accomplishments. We create a truly inclusive family where talent meets opportunity and passion becomes purpose by welcoming players of all nations, ethnicities, and skill levels.


🕟History

APU FC was established in 2015 as a grassroots initiative to provide structure and inclusivity to the university's football scene by a committed group of football-loving students. What began as friendly kickabouts and unofficial training sessions swiftly developed into a full-fledged club.

We are happy to have developed into one of APU's most active and well-respected sports groups over the last ten years, distinguished by our spirit of competition, sportsmanship, and community involvement. Our athletes have proudly and fervently flown the university's colors while representing APU in intercollegiate tournaments, state-level contests, and even international friendlies.

We have learnt from our mistakes, enjoyed our successes, and—most importantly—formed friendships that will last a lifetime. Our club's history is inscribed not just with goals and awards but also with cherished memories, commitment, and solidarity.


🎯Mission:

At APU FC, our mission goes beyond the football field.

We aim to:

  • Encourage a culture of excellence, self-control, and cooperation.

  • Give each student the chance to grow socially, intellectually, and physically through organized practice and competitive play.

  • Establish a setting where students can develop as teammates and leaders while learning things that go well beyond the field.

  • Encourage respect, diversity, and inclusion in line with APU's basic principles.

Whether you’re a seasoned striker, a first-time defender, or just love the game from the sidelines – there’s a place for you in APU FC.


👀Vision:

We envision APU Football Club as a top-tier university football team in Malaysia, admired for its professionalism, unity, and performance. We aspire to:

  • Continuously elevate our training standards with modern strategies and technologies

  • Develop our players into well-rounded athletes who excel both on and off the field

  • Foster long-term partnerships with sponsors, sports councils, and other institutions

  • Build a legacy that future generations of APU students will be proud to inherit and expand

Our dream is to be a club that not only wins games, but also inspires character, resilience, and lifelong passion for the sport
