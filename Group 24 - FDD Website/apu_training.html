🏋 Weekly Training

Every Thursday from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM at the APU Football Field.

Training is open to non-club members for RM10/session.

Sessions include:

  • Dribbling Practice
  • Passing & Receiving Drills
  • Shooting Exercises
  • Speed & Agility Training
  • Defensive Coordination
  • Small-sided Matches (5v5, 7v7)
  • Warm-ups & Cooldowns
