📜 Rules & Regulations

All APU FC members are expected to abide by the following rules to ensure a safe, respectful, and professional environment for all players, coaches, and supporters.

  1. Respect & Sportsmanship: Treat all teammates, opponents, referees, and staff with respect. Unsportsmanlike conduct such as taunting, insults, or aggressive behavior will not be tolerated.
  2. Attendance & Punctuality: Attend all training sessions, team meetings, and matches on time. Absences must be communicated in advance with valid reasons.
  3. Dress Code: Wear the official APU FC training or match kit during scheduled practices and games. No unauthorized apparel is allowed on the field.
  4. Substance Policy: The use of alcohol, drugs, or any prohibited substances during official club activities is strictly forbidden.
  5. Fitness & Discipline: Players must content-areatain physical fitness and commit to training regimens. Repeated lack of effort or commitment may result in suspension or expulsion from the team.
  6. Code of Conduct: All members must follow the official APU Football Club Code of Conduct, which outlines expected behavior on and off the field, including on social media.
  7. Injury & Health Reporting: Report any injuries, illnesses, or medical concerns to the coach or team captain immediately to avoid further complications.
  8. Conflict Resolution: Any conflicts or complaints must be reported through proper channels (e.g., team captains or committee) and handled with discretion and respect.
  9. Team Representation: Members are ambassadors of APU FC. Any behavior that tarnishes the club's image, whether on campus or online, will be addressed seriously.
  10. Match Conduct: Follow referee instructions without argument. Yellow and red card offenses due to misconduct will be reviewed by the club committee.

Failure to comply with these rules may result in disciplinary action including warnings, suspension from matches, or dismissal from the club.
