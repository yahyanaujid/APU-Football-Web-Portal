Frequently Asked Questions❓

1. How do I join the team?

Fill out the form on the Join Us page and attend the next training session.

2. Do I need experience to join?

No, players of all skill levels are welcome.

3. When are trainings held?

Wednesdays and Saturdays from 5PM to 7PM at the APU field.

4. Is there any fee to join?

Membership is free, but there may be optional fees for kits or tournaments.

5. What should I bring to my first training session?

Wear sports attire, bring your student ID, water, and football boots. Shin guards are optional but recommended.

6. Can I join just as a fan or volunteer?

Absolutely! We welcome fans and volunteers to support the club during matches and events.

7. How often do matches take place?

Friendly matches are held twice a month, and tournaments happen every semester.

8. Are there leadership opportunities?

Yes! Members can apply to the committee, help with media, events, and logistics.
