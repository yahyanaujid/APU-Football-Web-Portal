🏆 Club Achievements

Over the years, APU Football Club has achieved excellence both on and off the pitch.

🏅 Trophies & Championships

  • 🥇2024 Inter-University League Champions – Undefeated season across 10 teams.
  • 🏆2023 APU Invitational Cup Winners – Final win 3–1 against MMU Thunder.
  • 🥈2022 Sunway Sports Fest Runners-Up – Lost on penalties after 1–1 draw.
  • ⚽2021 ASEAN Varsity Tournament – Semi-finalists among 12 universities.

🏅 Individual Awards

  • 👟Top Scorer (2024): Ahmed Suleiman – 15 goals in 8 matches
  • 🧤Best Goalkeeper (2023): Mohammad Danish – 6 clean sheets
  • 🧠Best Coach (2022): Arne Slot – Recognized by KL College League

💬 Special Recognition

  • 🤝Fair Play Award (2023): For exemplary sportsmanship and discipline
  • 🌟Community Outreach Award (2021): For free youth clinics in Bukit Jalil
